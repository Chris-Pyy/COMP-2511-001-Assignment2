Overview of Programming

Computer programming is a way of giving computers instructions about what they should do next.
These instructions are known as code, and computer programmers write code to solve problems or
perform a task. The goal in programming is the create something that is meaningful. In the practical
world, programming can often used to automate tasks or increase the efficiency of tedious operations,
so it makes our day to day lives easier.
