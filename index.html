Overview

Computer programming is a way of giving computers instructions about what they should do next. These instructions are known as code, programmers write code to solve problems or perform various tasks.

The form of these instructions take depend on the programming language used. Languages can include very low level forms like Machine Language or Assembly, or very high level forms like Java.

Lower level languages are more complex and harder to understand, while high level language is very programmer friendly and easy to understand. High level is much preferred over low level for most instances because it is efficient and easy to maintain.
